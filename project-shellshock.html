Shellshock was initially intended to be a 2D side-scrolling shooter. I was developing it to teach myself the LWJGL library as a pet project and thought it would be a good way to learn the nuances of game development.

I quickly realized the enormity of the project after a few prototypes, and decided to suspend development of Shellshock until I had the knowledge and skill to create it the way I wanted to. To achieve that knowledge, I decided to create a framework to assist me in game development. I turned Shellshock into Void Attackers and developed Last Stand as a proof of concept of Core's capabilities.
