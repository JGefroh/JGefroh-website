RMS was a quick prototype that tested several approaches to the development of a record management system. It used Boostrap and GWT to create a sleek (but rather plain) interface so that various approaches in architecting validation and UI/UX could be tried. I really like the way it turned out, and the prototype gave me good ideas on how to approach development of other modules.
