At work I am developing a record management system that is very specific to a problem domain. While I am the architect and programmer, I have little influence over the requirements or GUI design. This can be slightly frustrating sometimes, especially when my own vision of the program goes against the proposed design.

I decided to take the initiative and develop my own record management system, which I will call RMS. I'm making it completely from scratch the way I want it to be made, without any interference. Let's see how it goes.
