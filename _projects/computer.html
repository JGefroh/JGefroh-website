I built my computer with the sole intention of playing demanding PC games. It died in 2015, and I haven't replaced it since. Specs:

  • CPU: Intel i7-860
  • GPU: Radeon HD 7970
  • Motherboard: ASUS P7P55D EVO
  • RAM: 8GB @ 1333mhz
  • SSD: Samsung 840 Pro (256GB)
  • HDD: 1x1TB, 1x500GB, 2TB NAS
  • PSU: Corsair CMPSU-750TX
  • Sound Card: Asus Xonar Essence STX
  • CPU Cooler: Noctua NH-U9B
  • Tower: Cooler Master HAF 932
  • Monitor 1: Dell U2711 (2560x1440, IPS, 27", 60hz)
  • Monitor 2: LG W2286 (1680x1050, LCD, 22", 60hz)
  • Keyboard: Razer Black Widow (2013)
  • Mouse: SteelSeries Sensei
