Weave, fly, and manuever your spaceship through hundreds of missiles and plasma shots as you fight a variety of deadly enemies! Spend points on upgrades like health and shield energy to keep you alive!

Last Stand was built as a proof-of-concept of my simulation framework's capabilities (Core). Everything was developed by myself from scratch - including rendering, input handling, timing, enemy AI, etc.
