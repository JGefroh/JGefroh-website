A real-time framework usable for simulations or games, based off of a component - entity architecture.

I designed and developed Core to assist me in making games. At the time I didn't know what kind of game I wanted to make, and traditional object-oriented approaches were architecturally too rigid respond to quickly changing requirements.

Core's dynamic composition design allowed me to quickly change my code to match the speed at which my ideas changed.
